    Goal 5: Achieve gender equality and empower all women and girls

    As part of the City of Los Angeles' commitment to adopt and implement the SDGs, the City has, where applicable, localized the national targets to align to city government plans and policies. More information on the localization process is available at this link.

    1. Home
    2. Goal 5

    Targets

    Indicators

    5.1 End all forms of gender-based discrimination
    5.1.1 Reported online Whether or not legal frameworks are in place to promote, enforce and monitor equality and non‐discrimination on the basis of sex
    • Non-Statistical
    5.1.2 Reported online Women's earnings as a percentage of men's earning
    • New
    5.2 Eliminate all forms of gender-based violence in the public and private spheres, including trafficking and sexual and other types of exploitation
    5.2.1 Reported online Proportion of ever-partnered women and girls aged 15 years and older subjected to physical, sexual or psychological violence by a current or former intimate partner in the previous 12 months, by form of violence and by age
    5.2.2 Reported online Proportion of women and girls aged 15 years and older subjected to sexual violence by persons other than an intimate partner in the previous 12 months, by age and place of occurrence
    5.3 Eliminate all harmful practices, such as child, early and forced marriage and female genital mutilation
    5.3.1 Exploring data sources Proportion of women aged 20–24 years who were married or in a union before age 15 and before age 18
    5.3.2 Reported online Proportion of girls and women aged 15–49 years who have undergone female genital mutilation/cutting, by age
    5.4 Recognize and value unpaid care and domestic work through the provision of public services, infrastructure and social protection policies and the promotion of shared responsibility within the household and the family as nationally appropriate
    5.4.1 Exploring data sources Proportion of time spent on unpaid domestic and care work, by sex, age and location
    5.4.2 Reported online Proportion of families with difficulty obtaining needed child care
    • New
    5.5 Ensure women’s full and effective participation and equal opportunities for leadership at all levels of decision-making in political, economic and public life
    5.5.1 Reported online Proportion of seats held by women in (a) national parliaments and (b) local governments
    5.5.2 Reported online Proportion of women in managerial positions
    5.6 Ensure universal access to sexual and reproductive health and reproductive rights
    5.6.1 Exploring data sources Proportion of women aged 15–49 years who make their own informed decisions regarding sexual relations, contraceptive use and reproductive health care
    5.6.2 Reported online Whether or not the city has laws and regulations that guarantee full and equal access to adults aged 15 years and older to sexual and reproductive health care, information and education
    • Revised
    • Non-Statistical
    5.a Undertake reforms to give women equal rights to economic resources, as well as access to ownership and control over land and other forms of property, financial services, inheritance and natural resources, in accordance with national laws
    5.a.1 Exploring data sources (a) Proportion of total agricultural population with ownership or secure rights over agricultural land, by sex; and (b) share of women among owners or rights-bearers of agricultural land, by type of tenure
    5.a.2 Reported online Whether or not the city has the legal framework (including customary law) that guarantees women’s equal rights to land ownership and/or control
    • Revised
    • Non-Statistical
    5.b Enhance the use of enabling technology, in particular information and communications technology, to promote the empowerment of women
    5.b.1 Exploring data sources Proportion of individuals who own a mobile telephone, by sex
    5.c Adopt and strengthen sound policies and enforceable legislation for the promotion of gender equality and the empowerment of all women and girls at all levels
    5.c.1 Reported online Whether or not systems are in place to track and make public allocations for gender equality and women’s empowerment
    • Revised
    • Non-Statistical
    5.x End discrimination against LGBTQIA+ persons and ensure that LGBTQIA+ persons have equal access to services and employment opportunities
    5.x.1 Reported online Whether or not legal frameworks are in place to promote, enforce and monitor equality and non‐discrimination on the basis of sexual orientation and gender identity
    • New
    • Non-Statistical
